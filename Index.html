Understanding the system that classifies military aircraft

What Are Jet Letter Designations?

Jet letter designations are a standardized classification system used by military forces around the world. These simple letter codes are assigned to aircraft based on their primary purpose, role, or function, providing a quick and clear way to understand what an aircraft is designed to do. Whether it’s engaging in air-to-air combat, delivering ordnance, supporting ground troops, or performing specialized tasks like electronic warfare or reconnaissance, these designations streamline communication, planning, and logistics within military organizations, ensuring that the right aircraft is used for each mission.
